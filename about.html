Mission

Our mission is to bring transparency and accountability to the informal service sector by building a simple, accessible platform where anyone can review service providers using just a phone number. We aim to empower individuals to make informed decisions, reward trustworthy professionals, and protect communities from repeated poor service.

Problem

In many cases, people rely on service providers from the unorganized sectorâ€”such as electricians, plumbers, and carpenters. These providers often lack an online presence, making it difficult for customers to share their service experiences. When poor service or unprofessional behavior occurs, there's no public feedback mechanism to hold them accountable. As a result, such providers may continue offering subpar service without fear of consequences.

Solution

This platform aims to bridge that gap by allowing users to review any service provider using their contact number. It will bring visibility to service providers who currently operate offline and enable customers to share both positive and negative experiences, including instances of fraud or misconduct. This transparency will promote better service standards and help others make informed decisions.
